About Surya Metals

Leading the future of steel fabrication in Kerala

Our Story

Surya Metals, also known as Binu Surya Metals, has established itself as Kerala's premier steel fabrication company. Founded with a vision to deliver excellence in metalwork, we have grown to become the trusted partner for architects, contractors, and developers across the region.

Our expertise spans across multiple sectors including luxury hotel construction, commercial buildings, industrial structures, and custom fabrication projects. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to quality, precision, and innovation.

Mr. Binu V. M.

Founder & Managing Director

Vice President, KSSIA Ernakulam District (2024-26)

With over 15 years of experience in steel fabrication and industrial leadership, Mr. Binu V. M. has been the driving force behind Surya Metals' success. His expertise in structural engineering and commitment to quality has earned him recognition as a leading figure in Kerala's industrial sector.

As the Vice President of Kerala State Small Industries Association (KSSIA) Ernakulam District, he actively contributes to the development of small-scale industries in the region, promoting innovation and sustainable business practices.
